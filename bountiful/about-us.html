About Us

Established in 1996 we pride ourselves on being Carlsbad's longest running coastal smoothie shop. Carlsbad bad is already rich with cultural, why not make it richer with a sweet treat.

We only use the freshest organic fruits for all of our specialty drinks. You can count on feeling refreshed and satisfied after one of our smoothies, and don't even get us started on their nutional value.

We are family owned an opperated by the same couple who originally started Bountiful Foods in the 90's. Since then we have open several sister ships in Newport, San Clemente, Irvine, and San Diego.
